🗑 Waste & Recycling

Collection Day

Friday morning, approx 6:30am. Put wheelie bins on the curbside in front of the property.

Black Bin — Non-recyclable

  • Crisp packets, black bin liners, food waste
  • Nappies, polystyrene, bubble wrap
  • Utensils, tools, sharp objects
  • Black plastic food trays, light bulbs, batteries

Blue Bin — Mixed Recycling

  • Food & drinks cans, steel cans, bottle tops
  • Empty aerosol cans
  • Boxes, card sleeves, card packaging, egg boxes
  • Waxed cartons
  • Bottles & jars (all colours)
  • Plastic drinks bottles, yogurt pots
  • Shower/bath/cleaning bottles
  • Light coloured food trays
A mixed recycling box is in the bottom kitchen drawer — empty into the blue wheelie bin before collection.
