Nottingham · Derby · Surrounding Areas

🏛 Historical

Nottingham Castle

£13 adult · free for under 15s
Rebuilt Ducal Palace with Robin Hood gallery, caves, and stunning city views. Recently renovated.
📍 Lenton Road, NG1 6EL

City of Caves (National Justice Museum)

£10.95 adult
Underground network of man-made caves beneath Nottingham — Anglo-Saxon tannery, WWII air raid shelters, medieval dungeons.
📍 Broadmarsh Shopping Centre, NG1 7LS

Wollaton Hall & Deer Park

Free entry · parking £5
Elizabethan mansion (Batman's Wayne Manor in Dark Knight Rises), Natural History Museum, 500-acre deer park.
📍 Wollaton, NG8 2AE

Newstead Abbey

£10 adult · gardens only £6
Lord Byron's ancestral home — medieval abbey turned stately house, 300 acres of gardens and lakes.
📍 Newstead, NG15 8NA

Derby Cathedral

Free
16th-century perpendicular gothic tower (second tallest in England), Peregrine falcons nesting, beautiful stained glass.
📍 Iron Gate, Derby DE1 3GP

Derby Museum of Making

Free
World's first factory (Lombe's Silk Mill) — interactive museum of industry, innovation and making.
📍 Silk Mill Lane, Derby DE1 3AF

Southwell Minster

Free · suggested donation £5
Romanesque and Gothic cathedral — exquisite 13th-century Chapter House with famous 'Leaves of Southwell' stone carvings.
📍 Church Street, Southwell NG25 0HD

Newark Castle & Civil War Museum

Free · museum £4.50
12th-century riverside castle where King John died. Civil War museum in the Old Magnus Buildings.
📍 Castle Gate, Newark NG24 1BG

Belvoir Castle

£20 adult
Stunning Regency castle, seat of the Duke of Rutland for 1,000 years. Capability Brown gardens, art collection.
📍 Grantham, NG32 1PE

Hardwick Hall

£15 adult · NT free
Elizabethan prodigy house — "more glass than wall." Built by Bess of Hardwick, one of England's richest women.
📍 Doe Lea, Chesterfield S44 5QJ

Loughborough Carillon & War Museum

Free
152ft war memorial tower with 47 bells. Museum tells local military history.
📍 Queen's Park, Loughborough LE11 3DU

Melbourne Hall

Gardens £7 · house by tour only
Queen Anne-style house with stunning formal gardens. Home of Lord Melbourne, Queen Victoria's first PM.
📍 Melbourne, Derby DE73 8EN

Calke Abbey

£12 adult · NT free
The "unstately home" — deliberately preserved in decay. Fascinating glimpse into a 19th-century aristocratic hoarder.
📍 Ticknall, Derby DE73 7LE

Bolsover Castle

£12 adult · EH free
17th-century "pleasure palace" — lavishly decorated Little Castle with painted ceilings, riding school, stunning views.
📍 Bolsover, S44 6PR

The Workhouse (Southwell)

£10 adult · NT free
Best-preserved Victorian workhouse in England. Chilling insight into poverty and welfare before the NHS.
📍 Upton Road, Southwell NG25 0PT

🌿 Scenery & Nature

Peak District National Park

Free
First UK National Park. Stunning walks — Mam Tor, Stanage Edge, Dovedale, Kinder Scout. 30–40 mins to southern edges.
📍 Multiple entry points

Attenborough Nature Reserve

Free · parking £3
226-hectare wetland reserve — 6 bird hides, walking trails, wildflower meadows. Kingfishers, bitterns, otters.
📍 Barton Lane, Attenborough NG9 6DY

Sherwood Forest (Major Oak)

Free · parking £5
Home of Robin Hood. See the 1,000-year-old Major Oak. Forest trails, visitor centre, annual Robin Hood Festival.
📍 Edwinstowe, NG21 9QB

Rutland Water

Free · activities from £5
One of Europe's largest man-made lakes. Cycling (23-mile loop), sailing, birdwatching, Normanton Church on the water.
📍 Oakham, LE15 8BL

Bradgate Park

Free · parking £4
830-acre medieval deer park — birthplace of Lady Jane Grey. Wild red and fallow deer, Old John Tower, rocky outcrops.
📍 Newtown Linford, LE6 0HE

Carsington Water

Free · parking £3
8-mile circular reservoir walk. Sailing, windsurfing, cycling, bird hides, playgrounds. Great pub at the visitor centre.
📍 Ashbourne, DE6 1ST

Dovedale & the Stepping Stones

Free · parking £3.50
Iconic limestone valley in the Peak District. Famous stepping stones across the River Dove, Thorpe Cloud hill, caves.
📍 Dovedale, Ashbourne DE6 2AY

Rushcliffe Country Park

Free
210 acres with lake, wildflower meadows, sculpture trail, playground, café. Great for a quick 20-min escape.
📍 Ruddington, NG11 6JS

Trent Lock & River Trent

Free
Riverside walks at the junction of River Trent, Erewash Canal and Cranfleet Cut. Pubs by the water, narrowboats.
📍 Trent Lock, Long Eaton NG10 2FY

Chatsworth House Gardens

Gardens £15 · house extra
One of England's finest stately homes. 105-acre gardens by Capability Brown, maze, water features, farmyard.
📍 Bakewell, DE45 1PP

Staunton Harold Reservoir

Free · parking £2.50
Peaceful reservoir with sailing, walking trails, wild swimming area, and a lovely craft centre nearby.
📍 Staunton Harold, LE65 1RU

Foremark Reservoir

Free · parking £3
Woodland walks, picnic spots, sailing. Popular with families for paddling and relaxing by the water.
📍 Foremark, DE65 6EG
